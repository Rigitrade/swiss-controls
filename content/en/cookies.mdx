## Do we use cookies?

Swiss Controls keeps this website intentionally lightweight. We do **not** use tracking, advertising or marketing cookies, and we use no technology that requires a cookie-consent banner. You can browse the site without accepting any cookies.

## What we use

- **Strictly necessary storage:** Any storage we use is limited to what is technically necessary for the website to load and function securely. It is not used to track you.
- **Privacy-friendly analytics:** We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymised traffic such as page views. This service is **cookieless** — it does not set cookies, does not store persistent identifiers, and does not collect data that personally identifies you.

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising networks, social-media tracking pixels or comparable technologies.

## Managing cookies

Because we do not set tracking cookies, no consent is required to use this website. You can still view, control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary storage may affect how the website functions.
