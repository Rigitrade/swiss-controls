selectionGuidance:
  - question: "When should you choose Inconel 625 over Hastelloy C-276?"
    answer: "Inconel 625 outperforms in high-temperature oxidizing service (>700 °C). Hastelloy C-276 is preferred for aggressive reducing acids and chloride environments at moderate temperatures."
  - question: "When does Super Duplex 2507 replace Inconel?"
    answer: "Super Duplex matches many Inconel grades for chloride pitting resistance at a lower cost in the temperature range up to ~300 °C — but loses mechanical performance above that range, where Inconel becomes essential."
  - question: "Why specify EN 10204 3.2 over 3.1?"
