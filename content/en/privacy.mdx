## Data controller

Rigitrade AG, Tannenstrasse 16, 8424 Embrach ZH, Switzerland. Contact: info@rigitrade.com.

## What data we collect

Information you submit through our forms (quote request, contact): company, name, email, phone, country, technical specifications, certifications, delivery details, message content.

## How we use it

To respond to your inquiry, prepare technical assessments and quotes, and follow up on the discussion. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data processors

Form submissions are routed through Formspree (formspree.io) — our designated data processor for inquiry handling. Formspree retains submissions for the duration of our service contract; you may request deletion at any time.

## Retention

Inquiries are retained for up to 36 months for follow-up and audit purposes, then deleted unless a commercial relationship has been established.

## Your rights

Under Swiss FADP and EU GDPR you have the right to: access your data, request correction, request deletion, object to processing, and request data portability. Contact info@rigitrade.com to exercise any of these rights.

## Cookies

This website does not set tracking cookies. Performance metrics (Web Vitals) are collected via Vercel Analytics in an anonymized, cookieless manner.

## Updates

This policy may be updated. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
