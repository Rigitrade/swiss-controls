## 3. Types of Personal Data

We may process the following categories of personal data:

- **Usage and Technical Data:** IP address, log files (date and time of access, requested URL, status code, browser type, operating system, user agent), device information and, where applicable, cookie IDs.
- **Communication Data:** Information provided through emails, telephone conversations, contact forms or other communication channels (e.g. name, company, position, email address, telephone number and content of the inquiry).
- **Business and Contract Data:** Master data (company, contact persons, addresses), contract data, quotation data, project information, technical requirements, invoice and payment information as well as business correspondence.
- **Project and Engineering Data:** Technical specifications, project documentation, drawings, system information, automation requirements and other technical information required for project execution.
- **Account and Access Data:** User accounts, login information, roles and permissions, where required for our services or systems.
- **Support and Service Data:** Support requests, error descriptions, technical information and documentation related to our services.
- **Application Data:** Information voluntarily provided to us as part of an application process.

## 4. Purposes of Processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

- **Website Operation and Security:** Providing, operating, maintaining and securing our website, analysing errors and protecting against misuse and cyber threats.
- **Communication and Contract Execution:** Processing inquiries, preparing quotations, managing customer relationships, executing contracts, invoicing and providing support.
- **Engineering, Automation and Electrification Services:** Planning, engineering, implementation, commissioning, maintenance and optimisation of industrial automation systems, control systems, electrical systems, digital solutions and technical infrastructures.
- **Digital Transformation and Industry 4.0 Solutions:** Providing software solutions, industrial data platforms, monitoring systems, analytics solutions and digital optimisation services.
- **Business Relationships and Business Development:** Managing relationships with customers, partners and suppliers, and handling cooperation opportunities, tenders and business inquiries.
- **Legal Obligations and Enforcement of Rights:** Compliance with legal retention obligations and protection, enforcement or defence of legal claims.

## 5. Legal Basis

We process personal data in accordance with the provisions of the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (revFADP). Where and to the extent that the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, processing is based, depending on the purpose, on:

- Article 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)
- Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract)
- Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests)

Our legitimate interests include, in particular, the secure operation of our systems, communication with customers and the continuous improvement of our services.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure the operation and security of our website and to provide certain functions.

- **Necessary Technologies:** Technically required cookies are used without separate consent where they are necessary for website operation or requested functions.
- **Non-essential Technologies:** Cookies and services for analytics, marketing or optimisation purposes are only used with your consent.

You may withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future through the cookie settings on our website. Our website can generally be used without non-essential cookies.

We store personal data only for as long as necessary for the relevant purposes or as required by statutory retention obligations.

- **Technical log data:** Generally 7 to 180 days for security and analysis purposes.
- **Business and contract documents:** According to statutory commercial and tax retention requirements.
- **Contact and communication data:** For as long as necessary to process the inquiry or maintain the business relationship.
- **Project and support data:** Project-specific and according to the respective business relationship.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures (TOM) to protect personal data against loss, misuse, manipulation or unauthorised access. These include in particular:

- Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Access restrictions based on the need-to-know principle
- Regular backups
- Protection of our IT systems through appropriate security measures
- Regular review and improvement of security measures
