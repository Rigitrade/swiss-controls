# Competitor Analysis — Quasi Systems

**Prepared for:** Swiss Controls website project
**Competitor analyzed:** https://www.quasi-systems.com/
**Date:** 2026-07-07
**Purpose:** Understand the competitor's content, structure, and design to inform a new, differentiated website for Swiss Controls (a new Swiss company with an Egypt branch in Automation & Industrial Control Systems).

---

## 1. Company Identity & Positioning

- **Main message / tagline:** "Engineering that changes the world" + "custom tailored electrical turnkey solutions."
- **Field:** Energy & Control Solutions — not pure automation. A blend of: product reselling + system integration + panel manufacturing + maintenance services.
- **Selling points (trust builders):**
  - 10+ years experience
  - 500+ customers
  - **Certified Channel Partner for ABB**
  - **System Integrator for Schneider Electric**
  - Owns a **panel-building factory in 10th of Ramadan City**

## 6. Visual Design

- **Colors:** Orange (~`#EA5B0C`) + dark navy/black + white. Clean and professional.
- **Hero:** Slider with images cropped into angular/diagonal geometric shapes — a distinctive look.
- **Sections:** Services on a dark "circuit-board" background with orange icon tiles; products and logos in carousels.
- **Details:** Pill-shaped nav buttons, floating WhatsApp button, contact form + Google Map at the bottom.

### Weaknesses / gaps (opportunities for us)
- Hero loads slowly (empty for the first ~2 seconds).
- **English only** — no Arabic version.
- "News" section appears neglected.
- Industries/sectors served are not clearly specified.
- No real case studies with measurable results.

## 8. Recommendations for Swiss Controls

### Keep (proven for this market)
- Same core structure: Services + Products-by-brand + Partners + Clients + Projects.
- Strong display of partner/client logos for instant credibility.

### Add / differentiate (the "Swiss" edge)
- Position on **Swiss engineering standards — precision & quality** (our key differentiator vs. Quasi).
- **Bilingual site (Arabic + English)** — Quasi is English-only.
- Clear **Industries** section (Oil & Gas, Water, Food & Beverage, Steel, Cement, etc.).
- Real **case studies with numbers** instead of a neglected "News" feed.
- Better **performance, speed, and SEO**.

### Consider removing / changing
- Heavy single-brand focus (ABB). Consider a more neutral / multi-vendor stance if that matches Swiss Controls' direction.
